Building Autonomous Data Pipelines for the AI Era

Many data pipelines were never designed for the scale and reliability modern AI workloads demand. Today, 90% of AI projects depend directly on data engineering pipelines, yet 30-40% of pipelines experience failures every week as data environments grow more complex.

To keep pace, organizations are moving toward autonomous data pipelines that can automatically detect issues, adapt to changing conditions, and ensure reliable delivery of trusted data for AI and analytics.

  • How automation and observability are transforming data engineering
  • Strategies for improving pipeline reliability and data quality
  • Best practices for building scalable, AI-ready data pipelines
